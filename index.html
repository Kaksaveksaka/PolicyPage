Privacy Policy for Knife Slice 3D

Effective date: July 12, 2026

Knife Slice 3D is provided by Silver Hook ("Silver Hook", "we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use Knife Slice 3D on Android and supported web platforms.

Information We Handle

We do not require you to create an account to play the game. The game stores gameplay preferences and entitlement state locally on your device, including progress-related settings, audio preferences, and whether you own the Ad-Free purchase.

Our third-party service providers may process the following categories of information when their services are used:

  • Advertising data: Google Mobile Ads may process device information, advertising identifiers, IP address, app interaction information, and ad performance information to deliver, measure, and help prevent fraud in ads.
  • Analytics data: GameAnalytics may process pseudonymous device and app information, technical or diagnostic information, and gameplay events such as level progress and feature use to help us understand and improve the game.
  • Purchase data: Google Play and Unity In-App Purchasing process purchase transactions for the Ad-Free product. Silver Hook does not receive or store your payment card information. The game stores only the resulting entitlement needed to remove ads.
  • Web-platform data: When the game is played through Playgama, the Playgama Bridge and platform services may process platform, device, language, gameplay, and account-related information needed to provide platform features such as ads, saved data, and game services.

How We Use Information

Information is used to operate the game, save game state, provide ads and ad measurement, process and restore purchases, detect fraud or technical problems, analyze gameplay, and improve the game.

Advertising and In-House Promotions

Knife Slice 3D may show Google-served ads on Android. We may also show our own in-house promotional creatives. In-house creatives are stored with the game and do not independently collect personal information. Google-served ads are subject to Google's data practices and controls.

Service Providers

We use service providers to operate parts of the game. Their handling of information is governed by their own policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy for Google Play and Google Mobile Ads.
  • GameAnalytics Privacy Notice for analytics services.
  • Unity Game Player and App User Privacy Policy for Unity In-App Purchasing services.
  • Playgama Privacy Policy when the game is played through Playgama.

Data Sharing

We share information with the service providers described above only as needed for their services, including advertising, analytics, purchases, fraud prevention, and platform operation. We do not sell your personal information for money. Advertising providers may use information in accordance with their own policies to deliver and measure advertising.

Retention and Deletion

Locally stored game data remains on your device until you clear the app's storage or uninstall the game. Third-party providers retain data under their own policies and legal obligations. You may contact us to request help with privacy questions or requests relating to data we control. See our data deletion request page for instructions.

Your Choices

You can manage advertising preferences through your Android device and Google account settings, where available. You can remove locally stored game data by clearing the game's app data or uninstalling the game. Purchase restoration is handled by the relevant app-store account.

Security

We use reasonable measures to protect information handled by the game. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information directly to us, please contact us so that we can review the request.

International Processing

Our service providers may process information in countries other than your country of residence. Those providers use their own safeguards and policies for international data transfers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the game or its services change. We will publish the updated version on this page and revise the effective date.
